â“ What Exactly IS Selenium?

Selenium is a robot that controls your Chrome browser. It can:

  • Open web pages
  • Find buttons and input fields
  • Click buttons
  • Type text into fields
  • Submit forms
  • Read what's on the page

In Waypoint, Selenium automates clicking around in Jira without needing a REST API.

ğŸ¬ The Complete PO User Journey

1

You Launch Waypoint

What you do: Double-click GitHubJiraSync.exe

What Selenium does: Launches a Chrome browser in the background.
2

You Click "Initialize"

What you do: Enter your Jira URL, configure settings

What Selenium does: Navigates the Chrome browser to your Jira instance (https://your-company.atlassian.net)
3

You Log In to Jira (CRITICAL!)

What you do: Enter username and password in the Chrome browser

What Selenium does: Watches and waits. It does NOT log in for you. Instead, it:
  • Waits for you to authenticate manually
  • Saves your session cookies in Chrome
  • Uses that authenticated session for all future requests
Why? Selenium doesn't need your password. Once you log in, the browser session is authenticated. Selenium then uses that session to interact with Jira as if you're clicking yourself!
4

You Click "Dev" Tab â†’ "Sync Now"

What you do: Click the sync button

What Selenium does: NOW THE MAGIC HAPPENS! This is where Selenium actually automates Jira.
